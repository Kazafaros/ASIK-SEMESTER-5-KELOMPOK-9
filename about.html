Our Purpose

Our mission is to predict the carrying capacity of marine ecosystems to support conservation efforts and ensure the long-term health of our oceans. We aim to provide actionable insights for sustainable marine resource management.

Our Methodology

We utilize a robust scientific approach, integrating the Habitat Suitability Index (HSI) with advanced machine learning models. By analyzing satellite data, we can accurately assess environmental factors and predict ecosystem health and capacity.
